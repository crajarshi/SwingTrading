üí∞ Trading Setup Explained

Entry Price: Current market price to enter the trade
Stop Loss: Exit price if trade goes against you (2x ATR below entry)
Target 1: First profit target (1.5:1 reward/risk ratio)
Target 2: Second profit target (2:1 reward/risk ratio)
Position Size: Number of shares calculated for 0.5% portfolio risk
Total Risk: Maximum dollar amount you could lose on this trade

  • Scale out at targets - Take 50% profit at Target 1, let rest run to Target 2

üìà How to Place Trades

Step 1: Open your broker (Alpaca, TD Ameritrade, etc.)
Step 2: Enter the symbol (e.g., AAL)
Step 3: Set up a bracket order:
  • Buy [Position Size] shares at Market or Entry Price
  • Set Stop Loss at the calculated price
  • Set Profit Target at Target 1 price
Step 4: Monitor the trade and adjust targets as needed
